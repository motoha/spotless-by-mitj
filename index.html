Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23/09/2024

Spotless By MITJ ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Spotless By MITJ mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Data: While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Location Data: We collect and process information about your location to provide features that rely on your location, both while the app is in use and when it is running in the background.

2. Use of Location Data

Why We Collect Location Data

We use your location information to enhance your experience in the following ways:

  • The Spotless by MITJ application requires a location validation because Spotless by MITJ application will take attendance based on work location use geofence library.

Background Location Usage

In order to provide attandance submission, we may need to access your location even when the app is not actively running (in the background). For example:

  • Your location require to activate submission button.

User Consent

We will always ask for your explicit consent to collect and use location data, including in the background. You can choose to grant or deny this permission. If granted, you may disable background location access at any time in your device’s settings.

3. How We Share Location Data

We do not share your location data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with service providers to enable certain functionalities (e.g., maps, routing).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your location information if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your personal data and are committed to securing your data. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your location and other personal information.

5. Your Choices

  • Managing Permissions: You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device’s settings. If you choose not to allow location access, certain features of the app may not function as intended.
  • Deleting Your Data: You may request that we delete any personal data we have collected, including your location data, by contacting us at itmitj@mitj.co.id.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
